Welcome to Al Salama Diagnostic Centre

About us

Al Salama Diagnostic Centre is a gcc approved medical centre for pre departure medical check up for expatriates. We are doing pre departure medical check up for gcc countries like Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, State of Kuwait,Sultanate of Oman, State of Qatar,United Arab Emirates and Kingdom of Bahrain.

Learn More

Our Services

The Executive Board of GCC Approved clinic to issue medical fitness for the expatriates travelling abroad for job and others, we are proud to offer a winning combination of personalise attention, the expertise of certified professional staff, state of the art equipments.

Vision & Mission

To protect citizens of gulf countries from diseases and spread of infection.
To make sure that expatriates are medical fit, with the objective that the expatriate is not exposed to any complication due its medical unfitness for the work recruited.
